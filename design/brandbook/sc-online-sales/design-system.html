Design Principles

01

Multi-Species First

Every component must work across cattle, sheep, goats, pigs, and equine. Species identification through color-coded badges, never through hardcoded layout assumptions. One lot card fits all five.

02

Copper Trust

Circuit Copper for primary actions and brand identity. Arena Navy for depth and data surfaces. Clover Green for success. The palette evokes the sale ring under warm barn lights.

03

Bid State Clarity

Three bid states must be instantly distinguishable: Winning (green), Outbid (red), Watching (copper). The buyer never has to read text to know their position in an auction.

04

Self-Service Booking

Sellers book and manage their own sales. Every form flow must be completable without a phone call. Field rep assignment is automatic. Settlement is visible from close to deposit.

05

Magazine Convergence

The Show Circuit Magazine and SC Online Sales share an audience. Design language bridges print editorial and digital commerce. Readership data informs auction targeting.

06

Auction Format Flexibility

Timed, racehorse-style, C2C, proxy bid, live view. The bid widget adapts its controls to the active format without changing the lot card or surrounding layout.

Atoms — Base Elements

Buttons

Six variants: primary (copper), secondary (navy), outline, success (clover), ghost, danger. Three sizes. Icon buttons for compact actions.

Button Variants

variant: primary | secondary | outline | success | ghost | danger

Primary for bid and main CTAs. Secondary for navigation. Outline for watchlist. Success for confirmed. Ghost for dismiss. Danger for destructive.

🔔

Sizes & Icon Buttons

size: sm (6/14px) | md (10/20px) | lg (14/28px)

Small for table/inline. Default for standard. Large for hero CTAs. Icon buttons are 36px circles for watchlist and alert actions.

Inputs & Select

Form controls with focus, error, and success states. Used across sale booking, registration, and account forms.

Input States

state: default | focus | error | success

Copper focus ring with 15% opacity. Error with red border and message. Select with custom chevron. Labels always uppercase 11px.
