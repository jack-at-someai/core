Company History

2020
CardVault founded by Chris Costa, Ed Kane, Randy Greenstein, Tim Bonito, and Scott Heigelmann. The sports card boom meets premium retail.
2021
First three locations open: TD Garden (Boston), Patriot Place (Foxborough), and Foxwoods Resort Casino (CT). CardVault establishes the experiential retail model: custom vaults, live breaks, grading submissions.
2024
Named "America's Best Card Shop." Inc. Magazine Fastest Growing Private Companies — 338% growth over three years.
2025
February 12, 2025 — Tom Brady acquires 50% stake. Rebrands to "CardVault by Tom Brady." Then: American Dream (NJ), East Hampton (NY), Wrigley Field (Chicago), SoHo NYC, American Airlines Center (Dallas), Mall of America, Mandalay Bay (Las Vegas). 3 locations to 11 in under a year.
2026
February 2026 — San Francisco flagship opens at Oracle Park (12th location). Sacramento, Brooklyn, and Palo Alto announced. National footprint: 9 states and growing.
