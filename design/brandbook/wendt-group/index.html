Company History

1986 — Kevin Wendt begins conducting live livestock auctions in Ohio.

2004 — ShowPig.com launches with the first-ever online show pig auction (March 21, 2004).

2010s — ShowPig.com doubles auction count. CattleConnect.com launches. American Shorthorn Association partnership begins.

2015 — Kevin Wendt inducted into Ohio Auctioneers Hall of Fame. OSU Distinguished Alumni Award.

2020 — COVID accelerates online auction adoption. ShowPig.com becomes essential industry infrastructure.

2024 — Sales Advisor Team launched. Kennedy Ventures partnership brings Showpig LIVE — hybrid live+online via CCi.live.

2026 — 6,500+ live auctions, 12,000+ online auctions conducted to date. 20K+ email subscribers. 4 platforms.
