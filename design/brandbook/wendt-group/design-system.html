Design Principles

01

Auction-First Hierarchy

Every interface revolves around the auction lot. Lot number, current bid, time remaining, and bid action are the four elements that must be visible at all times during a live or timed sale.

02

Agricultural Palette

Auction Red for urgency and primary actions. Wheat Gold for warmth and trust. Pasture Green for success and confirmation. Colors that feel like the sale barn, not a SaaS dashboard.

03

Real-Time State

Auctions are live events. Every component must communicate its temporal state: upcoming, live, closing, sold, settled. The interface breathes with the pace of the auctioneer.

04

Breeder Trust

Every seller sees the same professional experience. Typography is bold and readable at barn distance. Data is transparent. Settlement is visible from lot close to check deposited.

05

Multi-Platform Coherence

ShowPig.com, CattleConnect, Showpig LIVE, TWG Auctions. Four platforms, one design language. Color anchors and typography create family resemblance across all properties.

06

Glass Morphism

Frosted glass containers with 12-60px blur. Depth through layering. Translucent surfaces let the auction energy bleed through every panel and overlay.

Atoms — Base Elements

Buttons

Four variants: primary (auction red), secondary (wheat outline), success (pasture), ghost. Three sizes: small, default, large. Icon buttons for compact actions.

Button Variants

variant: primary | secondary | success | ghost

Primary for bid actions and main CTAs. Secondary for watch/follow actions. Success for confirmed states. Ghost for dismiss/cancel.

Sizes & Icons

size: sm (6/14px) | md (10/20px) | lg (14/28px)

Small for inline/table actions. Default for standard UI. Large for hero CTAs and bid buttons. Icon buttons 36px circle.

Inputs

Form inputs with focus ring, error, and success states. Labels above. Select dropdowns with custom arrow.

Text Input States

state: default | focus | error | success

Focus ring uses auction-500 with 15% opacity shadow. Error border + message in red. Success border for validated fields.

Select Dropdown

Custom arrow, auction-500 focus ring

Native select with custom chevron icon. Same focus behavior as text inputs. Used for species, format, and breed filters.

Badges

Status indicators for auction state, platform identification, and category tagging. Five color variants matching the brand palette.
