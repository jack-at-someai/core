Buttons

Filled Buttons

Primary actions. Steel blue for standard operations, forge orange for high-priority CTAs, green for confirmations, red for destructive actions.

Filled Variants

.btn-filled / .btn-forge / .btn-success / .btn-danger

Solid background, white text. Hover adds glow shadow. Active scales to 97%. Disabled dims to iron-600/iron-400.

Size Variants

.btn-sm (6px 14px / 11px) | default (10px 20px / 13px) | .btn-lg (14px 28px / 15px)

Three size options. Small for tables and dense layouts. Large for hero CTAs and landing pages.
