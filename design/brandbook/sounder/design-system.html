Buttons

Four button variants: filled (primary actions), outlined (secondary), text (tertiary), and icon (toolbar). All support default, hover, active, and disabled states. Accent variants available in magenta and cyan.

Filled Button

--purple-500 bg | #fff text | radius-sm

Primary action. Purple glow on hover (0 4px 20px). Scale 0.98 on active. Disabled uses neutral-600 background.

Outlined Button

--purple-500 border | --purple-300 text | transparent bg

Secondary action. Background fills to 10% purple on hover. Border lightens on hover state.

Text Button

--purple-300 text | no border | no bg

Tertiary action. Subtle purple background appears on hover. No border to minimize visual weight.

Icon & Sizes

40px circle | sm: 6px 14px | lg: 14px 32px

Icon button is 40px circle with surface background. Three size variants for different contexts.
