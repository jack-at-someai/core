Design Principles

01

Operational Clarity

Every pixel serves a dispatch decision. If a crew member, job, or route cannot be acted upon from the element, it does not belong. No decorative overhead.

02

Navy + Orange Contrast

Route Blue anchors structure and trust. Safety Orange signals action, urgency, and dispatch calls. The interplay between the two is the brand's visual pulse.

03

Tier-Driven Hierarchy

MVP gold, All-Star purple, Starter blue, Rookie green. Tier colors are semantic — they appear on cards, badges, tables, and dispatch queues. Performance determines color.

04

Dark-First Legibility

Operations run at 5AM and midnight. Dark backgrounds (#0B1120) with high-contrast text (#E2E8F0) reduce eye strain for dispatchers in any lighting condition.

05

Data Density

Crew cards pack 6+ stats into 240px. Stat cards show value, delta, and sparkline in 160px. Moving operations generate signals fast — the UI must keep up.

06

Collection as First Metric

Collection rate (billed vs. collected) is the health signal. Every crew card, job card, and stat card surfaces collection data. Green above 80%, amber 50–80%, red below 50%.

Buttons

Six variants, three sizes. Primary (route blue) for standard actions. Safety (orange) for dispatch calls and urgent operations. Outline and ghost for secondary and tertiary hierarchy.

Button Variants

btn-primary | btn-safety | btn-outline | btn-ghost | btn-success | btn-danger

Primary = route-600 fill. Safety = safety-500 fill. Outline = route border only. Ghost = no bg. Success = cargo-600. Danger = red.

Button Sizes

btn-sm | default | btn-lg

Small for table actions and inline controls. Default for forms and cards. Large for hero CTAs and dispatch confirmations.

Button States

:disabled | :hover (glow) | full-width

Disabled at 40% opacity. Hover adds glow shadow matching variant color. Full width for mobile/modal contexts.

Inputs

Text Input

.input + .input-group + .input-label

Background: --bg. Border: --border-light. Focus: route-500 border + 3px glow ring. Label is uppercase 11px caption.

Validation States

.input-success | .input-error

Success: green border. Error: red border + inline error message. Validation is immediate on crew, job, and route forms.
