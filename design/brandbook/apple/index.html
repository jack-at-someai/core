Liquid Glass

The new design language

iOS 26 introduces Liquid Glass — a translucent material that reflects and refracts its surroundings. Color is informed by the content behind it. It dynamically adapts between light and dark. Controls, navigation bars, tab bars, sidebars, and widgets all use this material. It behaves like real glass: specular highlights, refraction, environmental tinting.
