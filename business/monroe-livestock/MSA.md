**Payment Terms:** Due on the 1st of each month. Net 15. Late payments accrue 1.5%/month.

### Key Clauses

#### Lock-In Clause

24-month minimum commitment. Early termination requires payment of remaining balance at 50% of monthly rate for remaining months.

#### Auto-Renewal Clause

Automatic renewal at end of 24-month term unless either party provides 90-day written notice. Renewal rate: current tier rate plus up to 5% annual adjustment.

#### Data Ownership Clause

Client owns their operational data (sale results, consignment records, settlement data). SomeAI owns the Charlotte substrate architecture, protocols, inference rules, and cross-domain intelligence patterns. KRF knowledge base is jointly owned -- client receives full data export upon termination.

#### IP Clause

Charlotte OS, all primitives (NODE, EDGE, METRIC, SIGNAL, PROTOCOL), substrate architecture, and cross-domain intelligence remain SomeAI property. Client-specific configurations, dashboards, and brand assets are work-for-hire owned by the client.

#### SLA

- Dashboard uptime: 99.5%
- Response time: 24 hours (business days)
- Monthly meeting scheduling: within 5 business days of request
- Quarterly report delivery: within 10 business days of quarter end

#### Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary business information. Consignor and buyer data is treated as confidential client data. Anonymized, aggregated insights may be used by SomeAI for cross-domain intelligence enrichment.

#### Termination

- **By Client:** 90-day written notice + early termination fee (if before 24 months)
- **By Provider:** 90-day written notice (refund of unused prepaid months)
- **For Cause:** Material breach + 30-day cure period
