Industry

The show pig ecosystem

The US show pig industry is a $500M–$800M segment within the $25.4B hog and pig farming market. 12,400 NSR youth members. 77,000+ registered breeders across NSR, CPS, and ABA. Average sale price of $963 on ShowPig.com (2013 data) with elite genetics reaching $275,000. The industry runs on relationships, pedigrees, and sale rings — and the data layer connecting them doesn't exist yet.

Key technology gap: Most auction platforms in livestock are PHP/MySQL monoliths built 15–20 years ago. Pedigree data lives in siloed registry databases with no cross-registry integration. Sale records exist in PDF catalogs and Facebook posts. Genetic testing data (GeneSeek, stress gene status) is disconnected from breeding and sale records. There is no standardized data format across the industry. Sounder fixes this.

The crossbred problem: Over 60% of show pigs have no registered pedigree. Their lineage exists only in breeder memory, sale catalog sire/dam listings, and handshake deals. Charlotte reconstructs these ancestry trees by connecting data points across sales, breeding records, and show results. This is the single most valuable capability Sounder brings to the industry.

Vision

The auction platform becomes the industry graph

ShowPig.com has 20 years of sale data: who sold what, to whom, at what price, with what genetics. That data is the foundation for everything Sounder needs to build — but today it's locked in a legacy platform with no graph infrastructure.

The registries own pedigree data. The breeders own genetic knowledge. The auction platforms own sale data. The show systems own performance data. Nobody connects them.

Sounder's Charlotte substrate connects all five layers. Every animal, breeder, sale, show result, and pedigree becomes a node in a unified graph. The Wendt Group partnership gives Sounder distribution (20K subscribers, 125+ auctions/year) and data (20 years of sale records). Sounder gives The Wendt Group the technology layer that transforms ShowPig.com from a transaction platform into the industry's data infrastructure.

The same engine powering a $1.4M industrial service contract and a 12-location sports collectibles chain — deployed to the company that invented online show pig auctions.

ShowPig.com + Charlotte = the platform every breeder, buyer, and registry needs.
