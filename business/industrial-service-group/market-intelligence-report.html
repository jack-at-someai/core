1 Executive Summary

The global industrial air compressor market reached $11.05 billion in 2021 and is projected to grow to $14.45B by 2026 at a 5.57% CAGR. The top three players (Atlas Copco, Ingersoll Rand, Kaeser) control 46% of global share, but the aftermarket and parts segment ($1.74B) remains highly fragmented — creating significant opportunity for multi-brand service platforms.

ISG's position as a 16-brand, 40+ location industrial service consolidator places it uniquely against OEM vertical integration trends. This analysis quantifies the opportunity across equipment, aftermarket, and energy consulting service lines.

Methodology

Each source study was decomposed into structured data: market sizes, company profiles, pricing benchmarks, segment breakdowns, and trend signals.
