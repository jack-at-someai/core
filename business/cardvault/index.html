Company Profile

From hobby shop to national brand

2020
CardVault founded by Chris Costa, Ed Kane, Randy Greenstein, Tim Bonito, and Scott Heigelmann. First location opens as a premium sports card retail concept.
2021
First 3 locations open at high-traffic sports and entertainment venues, validating the venue-positioned retail model.
2024
Inc. Magazine recognition — named to Fastest Growing Private Companies list with 338% growth over 3 years. Named "America's Best Card Shop."
Feb 2025
Tom Brady acquires 50% stake — company rebrands to CardVault by Tom Brady. Brady's name, network, and brand transform the business overnight.
2025
9 new stores open — rapid national expansion to Boston (TD Garden), NYC (SoHo), Chicago (Wrigley), Dallas (AAC), Las Vegas (Mandalay Bay), and more.
Feb 2026
San Francisco flagship at Oracle Park becomes the 12th location. Est. $30M–$40M annual revenue. 100–200 employees across 9 states.
