How It Works
Four steps to owning
a piece of history
Unlike paintings that hang on walls, these instruments are alive. They are played, they resonate, they appreciate. Our model makes them accessible as investments for the first time.
01
We Acquire
Our team sources rare instruments from auctions, private dealers, and estates worldwide. Stradivari, Guarneri, Amati, fine French bows. Every instrument is authenticated by Paul Stewart Prier before acquisition.
02
We Securitize
Each instrument is filed as a separate offering. We divide the appraised value into shares at $20 each. Full provenance, certification, and condition reports are published for every offering.
03
You Invest
Purchase shares in any instrument starting at $20 per share. Build a diversified portfolio across violins, cellos, bows, and violas. Monitor appreciation in real time through our Charlotte OS platform.
04
We Sell
When market conditions are optimal, we sell the instrument at auction or through private sale. Proceeds are distributed proportionally to shareholders. Average hold: 3–7 years. Average return: 88.3%.

About PPP Instruments
Built on provenance.
Powered by Charlotte OS.
PPP Instruments combines decades of luthier expertise with modern provenance technology. Every instrument's lifecycle is tracked on the Charlotte OS substrate, from maker's workshop to concert hall to investment portfolio.
Chief Executive Officer
Dr. Brek A. Pilling, Ph.D.
Architect of the $100M target fund. Manages investor relationships, acquisition strategy, and market timing. Turns the science of instrument valuation into systematic returns.
VP Certifications
Paul Stewart Prier
Renowned luthier and the certification authority behind every instrument in the fund. Authenticates provenance, evaluates condition, and establishes fair market value. PPP's competitive edge.
VP Sales
Chris Johnson
Manages instrument disposition, dealer relationships, and auction timing. Connects the fund to the global market of collectors, performers, and institutions ready to buy at full value.
Charlotte OS Provenance
Every instrument is encoded as a NODE in the Charlotte substrate. Ownership transfers, certifications, appraisals, and market signals are tracked as EDGES, METRICS, and SIGNALS. The full lifecycle graph is immutable, auditable, and available to shareholders in real time.
Buy Below Retail
PPP acquires instruments at 15–40% below retail value, creating instant equity at the moment of purchase. Our average acquisition discount is 27.8%. This margin of safety protects investors from day one.
Expert Authentication
Paul Prier certifies every instrument before it enters the fund. Provenance is verified through maker records, historical sales, and physical examination. No instrument enters without full certification.
Living Assets
Unlike art on a wall, rare instruments are played. They are loaned to concert performers, recorded in studios, and exhibited at institutions. Performance history adds to provenance and value. These are not static assets. They breathe.
